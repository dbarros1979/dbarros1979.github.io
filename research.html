Research Interests

My main academic focus is the intersection of Bioinformatics, Computational Oncology, and Artificial Intelligence.

Topics I explore

  • Biomarker discovery (genes, proteins, microRNAs)
  • Probabilistic models for DNA/RNA motif analysis
  • Sequence alignment, scoring, and statistical evaluation
  • Machine learning models for cancer classification and prediction
  • Integrating genomic, transcriptomic, and clinical data
  • Computational pipelines for cancer research

Long-term goal

To contribute tools, datasets, and predictive models that improve early detection and personalized treatment for cancer patients.
